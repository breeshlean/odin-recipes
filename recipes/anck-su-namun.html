Anck Su Namun

Inspired by La Momisette

I really like La Momisette. It is a great cocktail for a sultry summer day. I like it so much that one chilly fall day, I decided to play with the flavors a bit. I wanted a similar taste profile but in a classic cocktail form.

Since La Momisette means 'little mummy', or so goes the popular belief, I named this cocktail after a memorable character from 1999's The Mummy.

Ingredients

  • 1.25 oz Tequila Blanco
  • 0.75 oz Absinthe
  • 0.75 oz Orgeat
  • 0.50 oz Lime Juice

Presentation

  • Serve up
  • Garnish with a half lime wheel

Steps

  1. Chill glass in freezer
  2. Prepare garnish
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker
  4. Add ice to shaker
  5. Shake for 10-15 seconds
  6. Double strain into chilled cocktail glass
  7. Add garnish and serve
