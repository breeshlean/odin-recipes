Eggnog

This is a single serving eggnog for adults only!

Most eggnog recipes are designed to share. And that is great! However, what if you are the only one who likes eggnog, or booze, or you are a Scrooge? Then this recipe is for you!

There are many eggnog recipes in the world and they are all good (mostly). This is just my take on it. You should definitely play around with it to suit your tastes and available ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1.00 oz Demerara Rum
  • 1.00 oz Sherry
  • 1.00 oz Demerara Syrup (2:1)
  • 1.00 oz Whole Milk
  • 1.00 oz Heavy Cream
  • 1 Whole Raw Egg
  • Grated Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Chill a fun mug in the freezer
  2. Add the first six ingredients to a shaker tin
  3. Shake without ice until the egg has emulsified (at least 30 seconds)
  4. Add ice to the shaker
  5. Shake for 10-15 seconds
  6. Double strain into the chilled mug
  7. Grate nutmeg over top and serve
